Pepsi

Pepsi is a brand of carbonated soft drink created by PepsiCo. It is sold in the United States as both a single-bottle and a 12-pack. The company was founded in 1886 by John P.eter, a former United States Army officer and a co-founder of the company. The company was originally called "Coca-Cola" and was renamed Pepsi in the early 1900s. The name was changed to PepsiCo in the early 2000s. The company was renamed to Pepsi in the early 2010s.
